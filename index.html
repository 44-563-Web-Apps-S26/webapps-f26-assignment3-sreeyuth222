How to Make Masala Chai

  1. Boil Water
    1. Add crushed ginger
    2. Add cardamom pods
  2. Add tea leaves and let it boil
  3. Add milk and sugar
    1. Adjust sugar to taste
    2. Let it simmer for 2 minutes
  4. Strain and serve hot
